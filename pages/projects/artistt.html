Artistt

When we start working in the market directly (especially in non conventional field like any Artistic area) we are surrounded with few major challenges like finding the suitable clients and getting the payment on time, finding people with similar and complementary skill set with whom we can build and sustain small business, transparency of financial distribution among the partners in that small business etc. With blockchain driven smart contracts we can guarantee timely payment and transparency in financial distribution among partners and also allow each member to trace the history of financial distribution and the company's public group chat with the help of a smart contract named "Crystal". This history of accountability also encourages investors to evaluate early stage startups more conveniently which might speed up entire investment process. Ease in starting and sustaining business during initial days determine the economic growth of entire country which is the real motto of Artistt app to empower people do what they love.

In order to understand the solutions we need to understand how we created these fundamental problems at the social level.

Economy

Problem
  • Don't let talented people find each other and form their own work groups easily so that nothing new could be done easily.
  • Don't let people get the right amount of money and right kind of services at the right time so that small businesses run out of money before their growth phase.
Solution as @artistt.app
  • Find people with similar/complementary skillset with whom we can build and sustain small business.
  • With blockchain driven smart contracts we can guarantee timely payment and transparency in financial transactions and financial distribution amongst partners of small business.
