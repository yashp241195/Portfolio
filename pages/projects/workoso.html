website : workoso.in

At @workoso.app, we can help people find work more easily and improve the ease of doing business

1. People can find jobs, freelance works etc and understand the demands of particular services according to their geography.

2. People can connect with each other and get access to their work reviews in order to understand the credibility of a person.

3. We allow people to create their own services and collaborate with other people on their own terms and up to a particular period with the help of "SERVICE ORIENTED" architecture for work (every work should be done by a service.) which solves the issue of speed of team formation.

For example. A team of 2 people providing service A of good quality can collaborate with a team of 3 providing some complementary service B of good quality can work together as a Company Z and produce better results with the help of SERVICE ORIENTED architecture.

It will improve the infrastructure more efficiently as the speed of going from step 1-6
